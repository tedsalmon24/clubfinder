What Is Club Finder?

Club Finder allows students of all degrees to discover exciting exhibitions happening around campus. No need to stare at your phone; EVENTS+ will alert you if time, location, and other important information changes. Students also have the chance to create a profile, edit persoanlity traits, and post their own events for others to join!
